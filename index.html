Gemstone Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game as your goal. There are four gemstones below.

By clicking on a gemstone, you will add a specific amount of points to your total sum.

You win the game by matching your total sum to the random number listed as your goal.

You lose the game if your total sum goes above the goal. The value of each gemstone is hidden from you.

You must keep track of your sum to figure out how many points each gemstone is worth.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of the goal and each gemstone.
